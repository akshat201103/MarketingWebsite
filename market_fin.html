Hyundai Venue N Line

Price: Rs 12.16Lakh onwards
Engine: 998 cc
Fuel type: Petrol
Transmission: Automatic (DCT)
Seating Capacity: 5 Seater 

Variants: 

The Hyundai Venue N Line is 
available in two variants: N6 and N8. 

Market introduction: 

The Hyundai Venue N Line was launched
 in India on 6 September, 2022. 

Engine and specification: 

Under the hood, the 2022 Hyundai 
Venue N Line is propelled by a 
1.0-litre, three-cylinder, turbo-
petrol engine capable of producing
 118bhp and 172Nm of torque. This 
 motor is paired exclusively with 
 a seven-speed DCT unit. 

Exterior design: 

Exterior highlights of the Hyundai 
Venue N Line include a dark chrome 
front grille, N-Line emblem for the
front grille, side fenders and tailgate,
16-inch diamond-cut alloy wheels, red 
front brake calipers, as well as red 
accents for the roof rails, side sills,
and bumper fender. 

Interior and features: 

Inside, the new Hyundai Venue N Line 
comes equipped with an all-black interior
theme with red accents, BlueLink 
connectivity, a dashcam with a dual 
camera, drive modes (Eco, Normal, and 
Sport), all four disc brakes, as well as 
Alexa and Google voice assistant. 

Colours: 

The Hyundai Venue N Line is offered in 
two mono tone colours, including Polar 
White and Shadow Grey, as well as three 
dual-tone colours such as Polar White with
Phantom Black roof, Shadow Grey with Phantom
Black roof, and Thunder Blue with
Shadow Black roof. 

Seating capacity: 

The Hyundai Venue N Line has a seating 
capacity of five occupants. 

Rivals: 

Rivals to the Hyundai Venue N Line include the 
Maruti Suzuki Brezza, Kia Sonet, Toyota Urban 
Cruiser, Tata Nexon, and the Mahindra XUV300.
